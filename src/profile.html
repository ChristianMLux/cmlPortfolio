Profile

Hi, I'm Christian, 30 years old, technical assistant and aspiring Junior Web Developer

I'm geek with diverse experiences in the IT. Technical assistant (software and Webdevelopment) and currently participating on a coding bootcamp.

My experience in IT is very diverse. I started as a teenager with self-operated game servers for MMORPGs like Lineage2 or World of Warcraft. In the course of this, I have dealt with the backend of the servers, but also with the frontend of the websites, with the organisation of my team and the work with the community.

I was able to gain my first official experience in the industry at the company "Safetec IT Systeme" in Bad Cannstatt. There I was responsible for the operation of the website of a charitable association (imaging4africa) and was allowed to accompany the company's own software during development.

In my private life, I have never stopped designing web layouts and implementing some of them.
After completing my basic military service, I worked in various industries to gain more diverse insights into the working world.

After this phase, I completed my vocational baccalaureate at the it.schule Stuttgart and my training as a technical assistant in the field of software and web development with commendation. In the following years, I dealt with the emergence of the new cryptocurrencies until I was drawn back to the actual industry. Currently, I am taking part in an intensive Junior Web Developer training at Coding Bootcamps Europe to refine my existing skills and build new ones.

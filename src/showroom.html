WORK IN PROGRESS

My history with Layouts & Code began in early 2003, when I started to browse through various online forums. In the forums, it was usually common to have a signature that was displayed below one's own posts. You could fill it with pictures. It was at this point that I had my first contact with Adobe Fireworks to create my first signature.

Around the end of 2005, I started playing MMORPGs. In these games there are associations of players. In some games they are called clans, guilds or alliances. In the time before Facebook, they needed forums to organise themselves. I started designing layouts for these forums at that time.
